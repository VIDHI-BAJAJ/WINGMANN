COLLECTING AND USING YOUR PERSONAL DATA

TYPES OF DATA COLLECTED – PERSONAL DATA

WHILE USING OUR SERVICE, WE MAY ASK YOU TO PROVIDE US WITH CERTAIN PERSONALLY IDENTIFIABLE INFORMATION INCLUDING:

  • EMAIL ADDRESS
  • FIRST NAME AND LAST NAME
  • PHONE NUMBER
  • ADDRESS, STATE, PROVINCE, ZIP/POSTAL CODE, CITY

USAGE DATA

USAGE DATA IS COLLECTED AUTOMATICALLY WHEN USING THE SERVICE. THIS INCLUDES IP ADDRESS, BROWSER TYPE, BROWSER VERSION, SERVICE PAGES VISITED, TIME AND DATE OF VISIT, TIME SPENT, UNIQUE DEVICE IDENTIFIERS, AND OTHER DIAGNOSTIC DATA.

WHEN USING A MOBILE DEVICE, WE MAY COLLECT MOBILE DEVICE TYPE, UNIQUE ID, IP ADDRESS, OPERATING SYSTEM, MOBILE BROWSER TYPE, AND OTHER DIAGNOSTIC DATA.

INFORMATION FROM THIRD-PARTY SOCIAL MEDIA SERVICES

IF YOU REGISTER OR LOG IN THROUGH GOOGLE, FACEBOOK, INSTAGRAM, TWITTER, OR LINKEDIN, WE MAY COLLECT ASSOCIATED DATA SUCH AS NAME, EMAIL, PROFILE DETAILS, ACTIVITIES, AND CONTACT LIST.

USE OF YOUR PERSONAL DATA

THE COMPANY MAY USE PERSONAL DATA TO:

  • PROVIDE AND MAINTAIN THE SERVICE
  • MANAGE YOUR ACCOUNT
  • FULFILL CONTRACTUAL OBLIGATIONS
  • CONTACT YOU (EMAIL, SMS, CALLS, PUSH NOTIFICATIONS)
  • PROVIDE NEWS, OFFERS, AND UPDATES
  • MANAGE USER REQUESTS
  • PERFORM BUSINESS TRANSFERS
  • IMPROVE SERVICE AND USER EXPERIENCE

WE MAY SHARE PERSONAL INFORMATION WITH SERVICE PROVIDERS, AFFILIATES, BUSINESS PARTNERS, AND DURING BUSINESS TRANSFERS.

RETENTION OF YOUR PERSONAL DATA

WE RETAIN PERSONAL DATA ONLY AS LONG AS NECESSARY FOR LEGAL OR BUSINESS PURPOSES.

TRANSFER OF YOUR PERSONAL DATA

YOUR PERSONAL DATA MAY BE TRANSFERRED TO COMPUTERS OUTSIDE YOUR JURISDICTION. BY SUBMITTING INFORMATION, YOU CONSENT TO THIS TRANSFER.

DELETE YOUR PERSONAL DATA

YOU MAY REQUEST DELETION OR ACCESS OF YOUR PERSONAL INFORMATION AT ANY TIME.

THE COMPANY MAY DISCLOSE PERSONAL DATA TO:

WE USE COMMERCIALLY ACCEPTABLE METHODS TO PROTECT YOUR DATA, BUT CANNOT GUARANTEE 100% SECURITY.
