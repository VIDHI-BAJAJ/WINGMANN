What are we building ?

At Wingman, we’re redefining modern dating. No endless swipes. No small talk loops. No waiting for replies that never come. Our AI-powered persona matching system goes beyond looks and bios it understands who you are, what you value, and the kind of energy you connect with. Each week, we handpick your top 5 most compatible matches, calculated through deep compatibility scoring. Then, instead of keeping you stuck in another chat window, we help you go out and meet. Just tell us when you’re free, and we’ll take care of the rest from finding your match to reserving a table that fits your vibe, taste, and budget. It’s dating made simple, smart, and human again.
